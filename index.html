About Higher Learning Hub

Higher Learning Hub is a mini project initiated by students at the Faculty of Technology, Wayamba University of Sri Lanka. Our goal is to address the challenge faced by post-2000 generation (GenZ) students who often lack clear guidance on their educational pathways after completing their A/L exams.

The main issue we identified is that many students simply follow paths recommended by friends or elders, often leading to educational choices that don't align with their goals and qualifications. As a generation that has grown up with technology at our fingertips, we created this resource to provide efficient, accurate information that helps students navigate their options.
